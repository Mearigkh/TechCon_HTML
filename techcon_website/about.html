About TechCon 2024

TechCon 2024 is the premier technology conference in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia, bringing together innovators, industry leaders, and tech enthusiasts from around the globe. This four-day event, taking place from July 24-27, 2024, is designed to explore the latest advancements and future breakthroughs in technology.

History

TechCon 2024 is the premier technology conference in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia, organized by the American College of Technology (ACT). The conference has a rich history, with this year marking its 4th edition. Since its inception, TechCon has been a platform for academia, researchers, policymakers, students, and practitioners to share their findings and discuss the latest advancements in technology and engineering

Mission

The mission of TechCon 2024 is to bridge the gap between business and technology through research. The conference aims to create a collaborative environment where experts from various fields can come together to explore innovative solutions and drive technological progress. By fostering knowledge sharing and networking, TechCon 2024 seeks to accelerate the development of cutting-edge technologies that will shape the future of Addis Ababa and beyond

Past Speakers

TechCon 2024 has hosted a diverse lineup of keynote speakers who are pioneers in their respective fields. While the full list of speakers for this year's conference is yet to be announced, past editions have featured renowned experts from various sectors, including:

  • Dr. Aisha Bowe, Co-founder and CEO of STEMBoard, a technology solutions firm
  • Dr. Kimberly Bryant, Founder of Black Girls CODE, a non-profit organization dedicated to empowering girls of color to become innovators in STEM fields
  • Dr. Neil deGrasse Tyson, Astrophysicist and science communicator.
